Rules For Posting A Question

You’re ready to ask your first programming-related question and the community is here to help! To get you the best answers, we’ve enforced some Rules:

  •          Search : Before you post, search the site to make sure your question hasn’t been answered.

  •          Summarize : Summarize the problem or a doubt you have in mind and include details.

  •          Describe : Describe what you have in mind and provide research.

  •          Display : When appropriate, show some code to reproduce your problem.

If you have read and understood the rules for posting a question , you are good to go!
